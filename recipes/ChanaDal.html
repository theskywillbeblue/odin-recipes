Chana Dal

A rich Indian lentil dish with a tomato and onion base

This suits for lunch or a side for a curry night. It's packed full of protein and fresh spices and the heat level can be adapted to you and your family's taste.

THIS IS AN INSTANT POT RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Split Chickpeas (Chana dal)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee/Oil
  • 2 tsp Cumin seeds (Jeera)
  • ½ tsp Asafoetida (Hing) optional
  • 1½ cups Onion (diced)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger minced
  • 6 cloves Garlic minced
  • 2 Tomatoes, medium, chopped
  • 4 cups Water
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Ground Turmeric (Haldi)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili powder (Mirchi)
  • 2 tsp Coriander powder (Dhaniya)
  • 2 tbsp Lime juice
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Soak Chana dal for 30 mins (do whilst prepping, drain afterwards)

  2. Start instant pot in sauté mode and heat oil in it.

  3. Add cumin seeds and asafoetida.

  4. Once the cumin seeds start to change color, add the onions, garlic, ginger and saute for 2 minutes.

  5. Add tomatoes and spices and stir. Add chana dal and water. Stir.

  6. Close the lid with vent in sealed position and set instant pot to pressure cook at high pressure for for 13 mins.

  7. After the instant pot beeps, let the pressure release naturally.

  8. When silver button is down, open, add lime juice and give a good stir, waiting for around 10 mins as it will thicken up.

  9. Garnish with coriander and serve with pickled beetroot and fresh yoghurt.
