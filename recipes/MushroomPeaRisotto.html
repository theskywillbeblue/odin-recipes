Mushroom & Pea Risotto

This creamy mushroom risotto warms you up on a winter night!

A dish made with parmesan, white wine and vegetable broth - the arborio rice melts in the mouth and warms your insides.

THIS IS AN INSTANT POT RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter (1 at beginning, 1 at end)
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • medium pack of mushrooms
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 ¼ tsp salt
  • black pepper to taste
  • 1 ½ cups arborio rice
  • ½ cup white wine
  • 4 cups (950ml) veg stock
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • ½ cup of parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Turn sauté on and add one tablespoon of butter & olive oil. When hot, add the onion and sauté until slightly translucent, about 3 minutes.

  2. Add the garlic and thyme. Sauté for 1 minute.

  3. Now add the mushrooms and sauté for 3-4 minutes until tender.

  4. Add the arborio rice and stir to coat.

  5. Pour in the wine & cook until liquid mostly cooks down. About 2 minutes.

  6. Stir in the broth, salt & pepper then Pressure Cook; 6 Mins Custom High

  7. The cooker will beep when the time is up. Carefully turn the steam release handle to the Venting position for a quick release (It will loudly spurt out lots of steam and some water). Once the Float Valve goes down you can carefully open the lid.

  8. The risotto will look soupy at first. Just stir and It will thicken up. Stir in the peas, remaining butter, and cheese. Taste & add seasoning if needed.

  9. Serve with optional fresh-cut parsley, olive oil, red pepper flakes, lemon wedges, and fresh cracked pepper.
